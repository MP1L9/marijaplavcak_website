Introduction

Problem

Foodbies is digital tool to prevent food waste by food donation or redistribution of food to sell at lower price products close by date.

The no waste food app has been gaining popularity since the epoch of being sustainable and carbon-free. We throw away more than 1/3 of all food produced. Each year, that amounts to 1.3 billions tons. Nearly 60% of the waste occurs at household level. Reaserches estimate that food waste from households, retail establishments, and the food service industry totals 931 million tons each year. Usually luxury food from restaurants are prioritized over products food.
